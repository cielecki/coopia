  1. A Rebel figure can attack a terminal (Health: 4, Defence: 1 Block). Apply +1 Block to the terminal defence results if it is adjecent to 1 or more imperial figures
  2. Doors are locked to Imperial figures
  3. The mission ends when all terminals are destroyed (Rebel victory), at the end of Round 6, or when all heroes are wounded.

4. Deploy Rebel Figures

1. Heroes enter through the enterance. If you decied with a different placement than the default one, update the map below.

2. Each hero receives one activation token and places it ready (green side faceup) on his Hero sheet. If playing with fewer than four heroes, they may receive additional activation tokens as specified on their “Legendary” or “Heroic” Reward cards.

5. Prepare Dice, Cards, and Other Tokens.

Place all dice, Condition and Supply decks, damage tokens, strain tokens, and condition tokens within easy reach of all players.
